Austin Soares is a product designer, photographer, and developer.

Freelance Projects

At Apple Boylston Street, I was a product specialist. While this isn't strictly in the design realm, I learned so much from this experience about customer relations, while welcoming guests during the holiday season.

Seniorlink

At Seniorlink, I joined and quickly led design efforts across a HIIPA-secure application for family caregivers and care coaches called Vela. I helped our team rethink a mobile communication experience with accessibility and scalability in mind for both users and our business. I also worked on updating the professional experiences which power coaching and support between our care coaches, our partners, and our families.

Chewy

For my sophomore role, I pushed into native mobile app design specializing in Apple's Human Interface Guidelines to lead design for Chewy's iPhone and iPad apps. I was deeply embeded in the product and engineering team and built processes, prototypes, and products which transformed our business offerings. It also helped to have a great manager during a transition from startup to a publically-traded company.

iFactory

One of Boston's oldest-serving interactive firms was my first stop in my internship turned professional career. In this role, I tackled interactive design among veterans in art direction, IA/UX, front-end development, sales, and project management. I designed for schools, healthcare providers, and some unique start-ups. I was also a member of over 80 project teams to integrate new learnings on accessiblity and storytelling to every client we worked with.
