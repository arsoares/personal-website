Work

Austin Soares is a product designer, photographer, and developer.

Seniorlink

At Seniorlink, I am researching, prototyping, and designing accessible web and native interfaces for family caregivers and care professionals. As the lead designer, I am now in the process of exploring new opportunities we have to update our experience across all channels by planning a complete design system overhaul, progressive web app, and scaled operations for our caregiver collaboration app, Vela and the professional interface.

Chewy

At Chewy, I designed and maintained the end-to-end native customer experience of the iOS and iPadOS apps. I built extensive design systems from scratch, a developer-handoff framework through Zeplin, over 150 prototypes (for iPhone and iPad) and even committed some of my own code.

iFactory

At iFactory, I worked on 80+ projects across higher education, publishing, healthcare and non-profits as an interactive designer from art direction to implementation. I loved having an opportunity to explore new technologies, operations, and chances to meet with our client partners to present work and lead discussions.

Freelance Projects

At Apple Boylston Street, I was a product specialist. While this isn't strictly in the design realm, I learned so much from this experience about customer relations, while welcoming guests during the holiday season.
